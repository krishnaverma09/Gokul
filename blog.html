Our Certificate and Online Program for 2025

At our Playgroup level, we celebrate every small step in a child’s learning journey. To encourage confidence and a sense of achievement, we provide beautifully designed certificates for various milestones—such as participation in story sessions, coloring activities, rhyme recitals, and theme days. These certificates make children feel proud and excited about learning.


Understanding the needs of modern families, we also offer a thoughtfully developed online program for Playgroup learners. This includes interactive video lessons, colorful worksheets, virtual storytime, music sessions, and activity-based learning. The content is designed to be age-appropriate, engaging, and aligned with early childhood development goals.


Our online platform ensures that children continue learning and growing even from home—supported by loving teachers and guided parent resources. Whether in the classroom or online, our goal remains the same: to spark joy in learning and nurture every child's imagination and curiosity.
