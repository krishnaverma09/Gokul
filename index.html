Play-Group

Our Playgroup program focuses on early childhood development through fun, interactive learning. Activities include storytelling, music, art, sensory play, basic language skills, numbers, and motor skill development. With a nurturing environment and child-centered approach, we encourage curiosity, creativity, and social interaction to build a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Music & Movement

Children sing, dance, and play with musical instruments, developing rhythm, coordination, and auditory skills. Music sessions also enhance memory, pattern recognition, and emotional expression in a joyful and inclusive atmosphere.

Physical Development

Through play-based exercise, games, and outdoor activities, children improve gross and fine motor skills. Activities like running, jumping, balancing, and climbing support healthy physical growth and encourage active lifestyles.

Our Global Campus

Even our youngest learners explore the world! Through our Global Campus program, Play School children connect with cultures through stories, songs, art, and virtual celebrations. It’s a joyful way to build curiosity, kindness, and early global awareness—helping little ones understand and appreciate the diverse world around them.
